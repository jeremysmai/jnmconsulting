Want to recycle with us?

Here's how you do it.

Gather your filament scrap (poop)
Failed prints, prototypes, supports, purged poop, basically any excess extruded filament you don't plan to print with, we want EVERYTHING.

Fill out our recycle request form Use the supplied form here to fill out necessary recycling details like approximate weight and best location for pickup.

Collection time! We'll schedule a date and time with you for pickup.

Sit back relax and enjoy a coupon! You'll recieve a coupon from us good for a dollar amount off of a full roll of our recycled filament! Seriously that's it. For those with larger demands, we'll personally work out a deal with you.
